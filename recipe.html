Momos


Ingredients:


Onion
Garlic
Ginger
Soy Sauce
Salt
Chili Sauce
Chili Flakes
Szechuan Peppercorns


Instruction :

Onion, Garlic, and Ginger: These aromatic vegetables are often used together as a flavor base in many dishes, including momos. They add depth and complexity to the filling's flavor.
Soy Sauce and Salt: Both soy sauce and salt are used to season the filling mixture. Soy sauce provides a savory, umami flavor, while salt enhances overall taste and helps to balance the flavors.
Chili Sauce or Chili Flakes, and Szechuan Peppercorns: These ingredients are typically used to add heat and spice to the filling. Chili sauce or flakes provide the heat, while Szechuan peppercorns contribute a unique numbing sensation and additional spice.

Idli


Ingredients:


Rice
Fenugreek Seeds
Salt
Water


Instruction :

Rice:
Idli rice (parboiled rice) or regular raw rice Urad dal (skinned black gram lentils)

Fenugreek Seeds: Also known as methi seeds, these are often added to the rice and lentil mixture before grinding. They add flavor and help with fermentation.
Salt: To taste, added to the batter after grinding.
Water: Used for soaking the rice and lentils, as well as for grinding the batter and for steaming the idlis.

Rajma

×

Rajma


Ingredients:


Rajma (Kidney Beans)
Onion
Tomatoes
Ginger-Garlic Paste
oil
salt
green chilies
water

Instruction :

Rajma (Kidney Beans): Dried kidney beans are soaked overnight and then cooked until tender. Canned kidney beans can also be used for convenience.
Onion: Finely chopped or grated onion is cooked along with the spices to form the base of the curry.
Tomatoes: Fresh tomatoes or tomato puree adds flavor and helps create a rich gravy.
Ginger-Garlic Paste: A mixture of fresh ginger and garlic, crushed or blended into a paste, adds aroma and flavor to the dish.
Oil: Typically, vegetable oil is used for cooking the onions, tomatoes, and spices.
Salt: To taste.
Coriander Leaves: Chopped fresh coriander leaves are often used as a garnish for added freshness.
Green Chilies: Finely chopped green chilies can be added for extra heat, according to taste preferences.
Water: Used for cooking the kidney beans and adjusting the consistency of the curry.
